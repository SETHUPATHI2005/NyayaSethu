About NyayaSethu

NyayaSethu is a legal information platform designed to make Indian law easier to understand and access for everyone.

Our Mission

We aim to reduce legal uncertainty by providing plain-language legal information, document assistance, and pathways to verified legal aid resources.

What NyayaSethu Provides

  • AI-based legal information support in multiple Indian languages.
  • Guided legal document drafting for common issues.
  • Legal aid center discovery and emergency helpline references.

Founder of NyayaSethu

NyayaSethu was created by SETHUPATHI K, a developer passionate about improving access to legal information in India. The idea behind NyayaSethu is to simplify complex legal concepts and help people understand their rights and available legal resources.

With an interest in technology, AI, and public information systems, SETHUPATHI K built NyayaSethu to bridge the gap between legal knowledge and the general public. The platform focuses on making legal information clear, multilingual, and accessible to everyone, especially those who may not easily access professional legal services.
